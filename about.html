What is BsStudy?

BsStudy is a free online platform that provides week-wise study notes for computer science and engineering students. Our goal is simple — to make quality study material accessible to every student, completely free of charge.

We understand that studying for technical subjects like Database Management Systems, Machine Learning, and Web Development can be challenging. Finding good, concise, and well-organized notes is even harder. That is why we created BsStudy — to give students a single place where they can find clear and organized notes for their courses.

All notes on this website are organized week by week, following the standard syllabus of major engineering programs including IIT Madras BS in Data Science, NPTEL courses, BCA, MCA, and B.Tech programs across India.

Who Are These Notes For?

These notes are designed for students who are looking for clear, concise, and exam-ready study material. Whether you are preparing for university exams, NPTEL assignments, or technical interviews, these notes will help you understand the key concepts quickly.

Our notes are particularly useful for students of the IIT Madras BS in Data Science and Programming program, NPTEL online courses, and undergraduate engineering programs in Computer Science and IT across India.

We believe that good education should be free and accessible to everyone. That is the core mission of BsStudy.

How to Use This Website

Simply click on the subject you want to study from the navigation menu at the top. Each subject page shows week-wise notes in order. Click the View button next to any week to open the notes in Google Drive as a PDF. You can read them online or download them for offline study.

We regularly add new subjects and update existing notes. Bookmark this website and check back often for new content.
